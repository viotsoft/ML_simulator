Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data ML Career Simulator (the "Service") collects and how it is used.

1. Data we collect

  • Account data: your name (shown on the certificate), email address and a password (stored only as a cryptographic hash — we cannot see your password).
  • Learning data: your progress — quiz results and completed modules.
  • Subscription status: whether your account has an active subscription.
  • Traffic source: if you arrived via a link from our social media, we store the campaign tags (utm) to understand which channel brought you.

2. What we do NOT collect

  • We do not store card numbers or payment details — payments are handled entirely by payment providers (WayForPay or Stripe) on their secure pages.
  • We do not use third-party advertising trackers or analytics scripts on the site.
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing.

3. Cookies

We use only essential cookies: a session cookie to keep you signed in and a language preference cookie. No advertising or cross-site tracking cookies are used.

4. How data is used

  • To provide the course: track progress, issue the certificate, manage the subscription.
  • To answer your support requests.
  • Aggregated, non-personal statistics (e.g. how many users signed up from a given social network) to improve the Service.

5. Data storage and security

Data is stored on servers of our hosting provider (Railway) with encrypted connections (HTTPS). Passwords are hashed with a modern key-derivation function (scrypt). Access to the data is restricted to the Service operator.

6. Your rights

  • You can request a copy of your data or its deletion at any time by emailing support@mlsimulator.dev from your registration email.
  • Deleting your account removes your personal data; anonymized aggregate statistics may be retained.

7. Third-party services

Payment processing: WayForPay (Ukraine) or Stripe — see their privacy policies for how they handle payment data. We receive from them only the payment status, never card details.

8. Changes

We may update this Policy; changes will be published on this page with a new date.

9. Contact

Privacy questions: support@mlsimulator.dev
